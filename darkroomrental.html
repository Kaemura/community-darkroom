For $15 per hour you can use our loading room, develop your exposed film, use one of our 5 enlarging stations, and develop your prints in our 6' photographic sink. The darkroom has a supply of B&W film and paper chemistry (Ilfosol 3 / EcoPro / Multigrade), but currently you need to supply your own paper. A staff member / volunteer will be present to assist with anything you need.

Our enlargers:

*Single 110mm Lens available for Super Chromega enlargers.

- Super Chromega D Dichroic II (35mm Negative Carrier / 50mm Lens / *110mm Lens)
- Super Chromega D Dichroic (35mm Negative Carrier / 50mm Lens / *110mm Lens)
- Beseler Dichro 67S2 (35mm & 120 Negative carriers / 50mm Lens)
- Beseler Dichro 67S2 (35mm & 120 Negative carriers / 50mm Lens)
- Beseler 67CP (35mm & 120 Negative carriers / 50mm Lens)

The Upstate Darkroom is only equipped for B&W film / print processing. You may develop color film if you bring your own chemistry.

Availability is currently based on when staff / volunteers are able to be in the darkroom to supervise. In the near future we'll offer Membership that will allow 24/7 access.

Please check our calendar before requesting a reservation:

After making your reservation you'll receive an invoice payable online or in person, and a door code for entry into Taylors Mill.

You can sign up by sending a scheduling request to 864-906-2954 or upstatedarkroom@gmail.com with the following details:

- The date and time(s) you are reserving.
- What services you would like to rent the darkroom for.
- If developing film, will you be bringing your own chemicals?
- If developing prints, which specific enlarger(s) you want to use, or any available enlarger.
