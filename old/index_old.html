What it is now

A 420 sq/ft space in the WRKGRP creative community at Taylors Mill. Featuring 5 printing stations, a 6' Delta II Photographic Sink, and a work/hangout space. Construction on the space is almost complete, and the darkroom will be fully functional very soon.

What it wants to be

A place where people of all ages and aptitudes can experience B+W film photography, developing, and printing. Bring your exposed film to develop and/or print, or walk in with nothing and we'll provide everything you need.

Darkroom Rental

Camera Rental

For $15 per hour you can use our loading room, develop your exposed film, use one of our 5 enlarging stations, and develop your prints in our 6' photographic sink.

Don't have a camera? We have a stash of film cameras donated by the community for beginners as well as experienced photographers.

Info / Sign Up

Workshops and Photowalks

We'll offer workshops to ease the learning curve as well as introducing cool and fun ways to use your camera. We'll also host photowalks at Taylors Mill and other local spots where you can practice using your camera, and your imagination. If you are an experienced photographer, we welcome you to host workshops or photowalks of your own, we'll help you schedule them and get the word out.

Membership

While all of these services will be available from the counter, we'll also offer membership through Patreon at different tiers that will include everything you need to get into film, discounts on darkroom time, and lots of cool extras available only through membership.

Other Fun Stuff

We'll have a gumball machine that dispenses pre-exposed film by photographers in the community, you'll get to develop and print their film or contribute your own to the machine, and connect with other local film-heads.

We'll collaborate with Pinpoint Creative Studio to produce cool laser cut pinhole cameras.

We'll offer up gallery space inside and in front of the darkroom for you to display and promote your work

We'll participate in First Fridays so the community can come and hang out around the darkroom, have food and snacks, free film, photo hunts and any other cool stuff the community dreams up.

Thanks for checking us out! Please consider contributing, we're counting on the community to make this happen!
