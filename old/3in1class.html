Supporting the film revival and making it accessible to everyone.

June 16th 3-in-1 Class

The focus will be on the basics of shooting, developing, and printing 35mm black and white film. If you have a working 35mm film camera you can use please bring it along with you if not we will provide one for you. We will also provide a roll of film for you to expose during the class. Or feel free to bring your own pre-exposed roll of black and white film to develop during the film dev. portion of the class. All other equipment and chemicals will be provided.

The class will take place on June 16th from 9am-4pm with a lunch break between film and print dev. Within 48hrs of signing up you will receive an invoice via email

Classes are first come first serve with a maximum of 5 persons per class

Location: Upstate Community Darkroom, Taylors Mill, 250 Mill St. 29687
