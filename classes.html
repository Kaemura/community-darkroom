August 18th Film & Print Class

The focus will be on the basics of developing, and printing 35mm black and white film. Feel free to bring your own pre-exposed roll of 35mm black and white film to develop during the film dev. portion of the class. All equipment and chemicals will be provided.

The class will take place at 10am-3pm with a lunch break after Film 101.

Classes are first come first serve with a maximum of 5 persons per class

The price for the entire class is $70, or $35 for each individual part of the class.

Taking any class will get you a coupon good for 10% off your next darkroom rental!

Location: Upstate Community Darkroom, Taylors Mill, 250 Mill St. 29687
