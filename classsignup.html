- B&W Film Development Class -
4 spots available

LOCATION: 250 Mill St. Taylors, SC 29687
DATE(s): May 26th
TIME: 10:30AM - 2:00PM

This class will teach you the basics of using a darkroom and everything you need to know to sucessfully develop black and white film on your own.
The day will look something like this: Introduction, setting up your chemistry, film development, lunch break while film dries, cut and archive, discuss results.

No previous darkroom experience required. Black and White film only. 35mm or 120mm only. All supplies including film, chemistry, camera, and or paper will be provided by the darkroom. (optional) bring your own roll of pre-exposed B&W film.

After attending one of our classes you'll be gifted a coupon to get 15% off your next class or darkroom reservation!
