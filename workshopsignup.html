- Cyanotype Workshop $35-

LOCATION: 250 Mill St. Taylors, SC 29687
DATE(s): Sunday June 19th
TIME: 2:00PM - 4:00PM

This hands-on Cyanotype workshop will introduce you to how to make these famous blue photographs. We will start with an overview of the process and its history and then move to the darkroom where we will learn to coat the paper. Mix the chemicals, expose the prints with the sun, judge exposure and wash & dry the prints. All materials you need will be provided just bring yourself plus maybe a snack and drink!

You will go home with multiple prints made with your own two hands and a personal cyanotype kit to continue practicing what you learned.
